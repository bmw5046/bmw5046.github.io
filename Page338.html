Some of our Surface Systems

Some examples

 

AMC-18 Used to reduce nonspecific protein binding, biocompatible.  Can be added to other surface systems as a conditioning agent.  Very stable, very hard to remove.

 

AMC-148 Used to impart resistance to acids and bases including mineral and organic acids.  Used in optical analytic devices, sampling devices, can be made very hydrophilic .

 

AMC-2148 Used to help stabilize delivery and prolong maintentence interval in fluidic transfer applications, reduces carryover and stabilizes drift in transfer and analysis applications.

 

AMC-2286 is a halogen and salt resistant surface used to mitigate and prolong the corrosion resistance of stainless steels  by mitigating halogenic attack and ionic transfer.  It is used with buffers, salts and plasma applications. 

 

AMC-Inertium™  is used in gas chromatography and gas transfer applications.  There are surfaces out there that approximate Inertium but really, nothing comes close to the conformity, consistence and quality our very specific and unique process brings to the industry.  In addition to price and quality, our turnaround is hyper fast, we have no issues with gas phase nucleation or hydrogen embrittlement. 

 

AMC-G AMXC has several ultra-hard surfaces for tribologic applications, as well as atomic diffusion resistance surfaces.  These are also used nuclear research and technology applications.

 

AMC-Ultra Deac  The AMCX deactivation surfaces are used to encapsulate surfaces to prevent interaction with environmental contacts, typically in sampling and chemical analysis but it is also used in packaging and drug and food production equipment.

 

AMC-SS1 is used to silanize glass surfaces.  We have immense capabilities for this and can scale up.

 

AMC-SS2 is actually a class of surfaces that bond specific moiety surfaces to glass and some metals for various applications.  These moieties can be used for analysis, and be applied to dissipate static, or improve adhesion for other surfaces.

 

AMC-SS3 is a class of surfaces that are used primarily for reducing critical surface energies, friction resistance and reducing the coefficient of friction.
