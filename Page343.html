We need to be notified of all shipments to AMCX unless it is a standing order or a bulk purchase order.   We try to send your order in the same manner and in the same packing it was sent to us; however, if the packaging is damaged or does not seem adequate to protect the parts we may replace the packaging.  Similarly, if the packaging is excessive, we may send it back to you in our packaging.   We can accept all common carriers and the postal service, we return orders via FedEX, UPS or the USPS. 

 

We will reject radioactive parts, we do not have the capability or offer remediation of parts contaminated with hazardous materials, including biohazards.
